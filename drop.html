Water waste is a growing challenge

Traditional faucets in Algeria waste water every day due to uncontrolled flow, long temperature waiting time, and lack of awareness.

  • Uncontrolled high flow from open taps.
  • Litres lost while waiting for hot water.
  • No automation or feedback on tank level.
  • Water bills increase without clear reason.

DROPLY brings together faucet control and reservoir management in a simple, affordable system that fits existing installations.

  • Detect abnormal behaviour that may indicate leaks.
  • Promote sustainable water habits for households and communities.
HOW DOES IT WORK?

DROPLY faucet tip + reservoir sensor

A simple mechanical controller on the tap and a smart sensor on the tank work together to avoid waste and keep your water available when you need it.

1. Flow controller at the faucet

A low-cost plastic head mounted on the faucet. It is not electronic, which keeps it affordable and robust.

  • Three modes: full flow, eco flow, and shower/spray mode.
  • Universal adapters to fit most Algerian faucets.
  • Reduces splashing and unnecessary high flow.
  • Easy to install without a plumber.

2. Smart sensor on the reservoir

A sensor installed on the water reservoir measures level in real time and sends data to our app / server for monitoring and alerts.

  • Tank level monitoring (e.g. percentage remaining).
  • Notifications when the tank is low or abnormally empty.
  • Leak/anomaly detection using a simple AI model.
  • Historical view to understand consumption over time.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Some common questions your customers and investors might ask about the DROPLY system.

Do I need a plumber to install DROPLY?

The flow controller can usually be installed by the user in a few minutes using the provided adapters. The reservoir sensor may require basic drilling or mounting; we recommend technician support for the first installations.

Does the flow controller need electricity?

No. The controller head is purely mechanical and made of plastic. It does not require power, which makes it cheaper and more robust.

How do I receive alerts from the reservoir sensor?

The prototype sends data to a simple mobile/web dashboard. In the future, you can connect it to WhatsApp or SMS for notifications when the level is low or when an anomaly is detected.

Can I buy only one of the products?

Yes. You can start with just the flow controller, just the sensor, or both. The full system offers the best optimisation, but each product already creates value on its own.
